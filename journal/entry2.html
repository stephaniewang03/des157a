Overlays Design Pattern Research

Posted on

10/21/2024

The article emphasizes how modals can become disruptive if overused or poorly implemented. While modals are intended to simplify the UI by focusing user attention or gathering input, they can frustrate users by interrupting their workflow, like a pop-up.

What stood out to me was the strong focus on accessibility and usability considerations. It's important to incorporate an escape hatch with clear controls, such as a close button or the ability to click outside the modal. Keyboard accessibility is also crucial, ensuring that users can navigate and close modals without relying solely on a mouse. User experience should always prioritize minimizing frustration while maintaining focus on key content.
